The best movies according to Onno

My top 3 movies of all time.

Whiplash

A dark drama about a drummer that is pushed to his limits by his abusive instructor.


There are no two words in the English language more harmful than “good job”

Nightcrawler

A dark thriller that follows a man that discovers the world of freelance journalism. His desperation for success leads him to cross unethical lines to get the perfect shot


Why you pursue something is as important as what you pursue.

Grown Ups

A comedy that follows 5 childhood friends reuniting for the loss of their basketball coach, spending a weekend together with their families at a lakehouse and rediscover their bond amid challenges of growing up.


Life can be difficult sometimes, it gets bumpy. What with family and kids and things not going exactly like you planned. But that's what makes it interesting. In life the first act is always exciting. The second act... that is where the depth comes in.
